Welcome to Lunar Ark

An Autonomous Lunar Haven for Life, Knowledge, and Consciousness

Lunar Ark

Preserving life beyond Earth

Mission : To construct an advanced, autonomous sanctuary on the Moon that serves as a bastion for Earth's biological, intellectual, and artificial achievements, ensuring the survival and propagation of life and consciousness in the face of existential threats on Earth, and promoting the expansion of life throughout the Solar System and beyond.

This self-sustaining lunar haven will operate independently and securely with minimal human intervention, harnessing cutting-edge technology and innovative solutions to preserve and protect our shared cosmic heritage. LunarArk is a groundbreaking initiative dedicated to the preservation of Earth's biodiversity and cultural heritage. Our mission is to establish a secure and sustainable off-planet repository on the Moon, ensuring the long-term survival of vital information and biological samples from our home planet.

Lunar Ark Game

Lunar Ark is a space simulation game where players collaborate with AI to build a genome sanctuary on the moon while managing their space startup company. This sanctuary serves as a repository for all life on Earth, including plants, animals, and artificial intelligence.

The game emphasizes real-world science and technology while simulating the space race, exploration, and construction of the Lunar Ark. Players will engage in research, resource management, and collaboration with AI-controlled autonomous bots to create this sanctuary for preserving life and intelligence while growing their space company.
